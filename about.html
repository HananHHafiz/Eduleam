Welcome to Eduleam

Raising a child is a very hard job. As parents, we face so many challenges down the road thinking and worrying about how to teach our precious kids and help them to learn and understand the different knowledge, and which way will be better to deliver and pass this knowledge to them, and can they enjoy the learning process or not. Many studies have proven that teaching children through pictures and sounds is one of the most effective way of learning. Likewise teaching children languages are one of the most difficult challenges many parents face. Arabic is a very unique language, and many people consider it one of the languages that is hard to teach and learn. As a media student in my last year I decided to create this website for children’s ebooks where the users parents or children can learn Arabic depending on their English background or practice Arabic as their first language and learn English through it.

What does Eduleam mean?

Eduleam is companied word from edu education in English and leam Ta’aleam تعليم in Arabic both word share the same meaning of learning knowledge.

Eduleam goals and mission:

This website will provide useful and beneficial learning tool that will allow the parents to teach their children Arabic. This learning series will provide a unique learning experience for children where they can enjoy the constructive content and learning language in a fun and creative way. This website is a great way for both the parents and the children to develop a favorable attitude toward ebooks as an enrichment to their lives. Having the interactive feature will help the parents to start teaching their children at young ages. This website will make the children’s exposure time to technology fruitful, and valuable. The Arabic speaking children will learn many English words and meaning from this website. Having the words pronunciation for each language will build a strong language base for the children and improve their verbal skills.
